# Superstore Sales Performance Analysis (Power BI)

## 📊 Objective
Analyze sales performance, profitability, and customer segmentation to identify key business insights and revenue drivers.

## 📂 Dataset
- Source: Sample Superstore Dataset (Kaggle)

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel / CSV dataset

## 📈 Key Insights
- Technology category generates the highest sales (~836K)
- Phones and Chairs are top-selling products
- Copiers yield high profit margins despite lower sales volume
- Consumer segment contributes ~50% of total revenue
- West region leads in total sales performance

## 📉 Business Metrics
- Total Sales: $2.30M  
- Total Profit: $286K  
- Profit Margin: 12.47%

## 💡 Recommendations
- Focus on high-margin products (e.g., Copiers)
- Optimize pricing for loss-making items (e.g., Tables)
- Invest more in high-performing regions (West)
- Target Consumer segment for growth
